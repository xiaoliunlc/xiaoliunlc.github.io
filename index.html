Xiao LIU

Dr. Xiao LIU (刘啸 in Chinese) is a researcher in the NLC group of MSRA. His current research interests include NLP, multimodality and reasoning. He obtained his B.S. degree (June 2016) and Ph.D. degree (June 2022) from BIT and was awarded the “Excellent Doctoral Dissertation” of CIPS in 2023 (now called “Doctoral Dissertation Incentive Plan”) and of BIT in 2022.

Please visit and star our open-source project ProphetNet on generation and SimXNS on retrieval. We are hiring NLP interns! If you’re interested, please contact me through email (xiao.liu.msrasia AT microsoft.com).
